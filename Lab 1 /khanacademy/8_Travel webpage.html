More information:

Jamaica has white sand beaches, clear blue water, and palm trees. You can swim, snorkel, and relax in the sun. The food is spicy and tasty. Try jerk chicken and fresh fruit!

At night, you can listen to reggae music and dance. The culture is rich and colorful. Everyone speaks English so it's easy to talk to people.
